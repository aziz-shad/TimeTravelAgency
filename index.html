ABOUT ALPS

The Apls are the highest and most extensive mountain range system that lies entirely in Europe, separating Southern from Central and Western Europe and stretching approximately 1,200 kilometers across eight Apline countries: France, Switzerland, Italy, Monaco, Liechtenstein, Austria, Germany and Solovenia.

To make the most of your vacation experience, you'll want to plan your itinerary and activities to take advantage of the best wearther and optimal conditions. Remember, the Apls is located at a latitude 46° and 47° north.
